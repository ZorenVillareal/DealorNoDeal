1 Deal or No Deal is a game show where you have the chance to win a large cash prize.

2 At the beginning of the game, there are 26 briefcases, each containing a hidden amount of money. The amounts range from $1 to $2,000,000.

3 You will select one briefcase as your own, which remains unopened throughout the game.

4 The remaining briefcases are opened one by one, revealing the amounts of money inside.

5 After a certain number of briefcases are opened, the "banker" will make you an offer to buy your unopened briefcase.

6 You have to decide whether to accept the banker's offer ("Deal") or keep playing and hope that your unopened briefcase contains a higher amount of money ("No Deal").

7 The game continues until you either accept a deal or open all the briefcases.

8 If you decide to continue playing, the banker will make new offers after each round of briefcase openings.

9 At the end of the game, if you haven't accepted any offers, you will keep the amount of money in your original briefcase.

10 The objective of the game is to try and get the highest possible amount of money by making strategic decisions on when to accept deals and when to keep playing.
